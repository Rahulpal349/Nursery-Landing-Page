Plant Care Tips

Everything you need to keep your plants thriving

💧

Watering Guide

Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Stick your finger an inch into the soil—if it's dry, it's time to water. Overwatering is the most common cause of plant death.

☀️

Light Requirements

Understand your plant's light needs. South-facing windows provide bright light, north-facing offers low light. Most tropical plants thrive in bright, indirect light.

🌡️

Temperature

Most houseplants prefer temperatures between 18-30°C. Keep plants away from cold drafts, heating vents, and air conditioning units that can cause stress.

🌱

Soil & Repotting

Use well-draining potting mix appropriate for your plant type. Repot when roots start circling the pot or emerging from drainage holes, typically every 1-2 years.

✂️

Pruning & Maintenance

Regular pruning encourages bushier growth and removes dead or yellowing leaves. Wipe leaves with a damp cloth to remove dust and help photosynthesis.

💫

Humidity Needs

Tropical plants love humidity. Group plants together, use a pebble tray with water, or mist regularly. A humidifier can be a great investment for plant health.
